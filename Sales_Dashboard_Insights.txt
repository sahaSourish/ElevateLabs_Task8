Superstore Sales Dashboard Insights
-----------------------------------

1. The West region consistently recorded the highest total sales among all regions.
2. The Technology category led all other product categories in terms of sales volume.
3. Sales showed a noticeable peak during the months of November and December, suggesting a strong holiday season impact.
4. Despite high sales, the Office Supplies category had lower average profit margins compared to Technology.

These insights were derived after analyzing the cleaned dataset using Power BI visualizations including line, bar, and donut charts.
