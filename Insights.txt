Insights from Superstore Sales Dashboard
------------------------------------------

1. The West region consistently had the highest sales across the months.
2. Technology was the top-performing product category in terms of both sales and profit.
3. Sales peaked in November and December, suggesting strong year-end performance.
4. The South region had the lowest overall sales, which might be a potential area for improvement.
5. Despite high sales, the Office Supplies category had lower average profit margins compared to Technology.

These insights were derived after analyzing the cleaned dataset using Power BI visualizations including line, bar, and donut charts.
